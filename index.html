About Radhika

Welcome to Radhika's Indian Kitchen! I'm passionate about preserving and sharing the rich culinary heritage of India. From the spicy curries of South India to the hearty dishes of Punjab, from Bengali sweets to Gujarati snacks - I explore it all!

My journey started in my grandmother's kitchen, where I learned that food is not just about taste, but about love, tradition, and bringing people together. Through my YouTube channel, I share authentic recipes, cooking tips, and the stories behind each dish.

Every recipe comes with easy-to-follow instructions, ingredient substitutions for international viewers, and the cultural significance of each dish. Let's cook together and celebrate the diversity of Indian cuisine!
